AI Computer Vision System

Applying deep learning to classify heritage structures with high accuracy.

Problem

Identifying and classifying heritage structures manually is difficult due to variations in lighting, angles, and environmental conditions.

Solution

Developed a deep learning-based classification system capable of identifying heritage structures from images with high accuracy.

Approach

  • Collected and processed a dataset of 2600+ images
  • Applied preprocessing techniques including normalization and augmentation
  • Used transfer learning with MobileNetV2 and DenseNet

Architecture

  • Data preprocessing pipeline
  • Model training and validation layer
  • Flask-based interface for real-time predictions

Tech Stack

Python, TensorFlow, Deep Learning, Flask

Challenges

Handling variations in lighting, noise, and inconsistent image quality required careful data preprocessing and augmentation strategies.

Impact

Achieved 96–97% classification accuracy and contributed to digital preservation of cultural heritage through automation.

Key Learnings

Gained hands-on experience in model training, transfer learning, and deploying ML models into real-world applications.
